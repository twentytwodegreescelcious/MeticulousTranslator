start - Begin conversation with me.
greet - Kindly asks me to welcome you.
setlanguage - I'll translate all the stuff to this language.
translate - I'll destroy the Universe, maybe. Or I could translate what you send me. Mind the 50% chance of the Apocalypse.
availablelanguages - I'll show the list of languages that I know and can translate to.
newtopic - Start a new topic.
addword - I'll save the word and translation for it. The format is "word - translation".
getwordsfortopic - I'll list all the words that relate to the specified topic.
closetopic - Tells me that you are done with the words related to current topic.
